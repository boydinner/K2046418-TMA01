What is XML?

XML, short for extensible markup language, is a markup language format that allows users to store  and transport information in a structured way.

The ‘extensible’ part means that you can use your own tags and you’re not limited to what you can write.Tags can be defined however they want so that information can be exchanged freely with others. One of its strengths is that it is machine-readable and human-readable.

Example

<birthdayParty>

 <guestList>

    <name> Sam </name>

   </guestList>

   <guestList>

    <name> Michael</name>

   </guestList>

   <vipList>

    <name> Malcolm</name>

   </vipList>

</birthdayParty>
